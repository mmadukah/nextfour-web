Nextfour — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: 7 May 2026

This page explains how to permanently delete your Nextfour account and all associated personal data. Nextfour respects your right to erasure under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open the Nextfour app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm the action when prompted.

Your account and personal data will be removed from our database immediately. Some derived records (e.g. session history that other players rely on for their own match statistics) may be anonymised rather than deleted, so other players' historical data is not corrupted.

Option 2 — Email request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to kreed_uk@hotmail.com with the subject line "Nextfour account deletion request" and include:

  • The email address associated with your Nextfour account.
  • The full name on your account.

We will verify your identity and complete the deletion within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email when the deletion is finished.

What gets deleted:
  • Your profile (name, email, profile picture, gender, date of birth).
  • Your authentication record (Supabase Auth and any linked Facebook / Google / Apple identity).
  • Your subscription association in RevenueCat (any active App Store / Play Store subscription must be cancelled separately by you).
  • Your club memberships, friendships, push notification tokens, and personal stats.
  • Any shadow / guest profiles you created as a host.
What may be retained (anonymised):
  • Aggregate match results and session history kept for the integrity of other players' statistics. Your name is replaced with a generic identifier and your account is no longer linked to those records.
  • Records we are legally required to retain (e.g. financial transaction logs).

Cancelling subscriptions

Deleting your Nextfour account does not automatically cancel a paid subscription. To stop being billed, cancel the subscription via:

  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Nextfour.
  • Android: Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Nextfour.

Questions

For any questions about deletion or the data we hold on you, contact kreed_uk@hotmail.com. See also the Privacy Policy.
